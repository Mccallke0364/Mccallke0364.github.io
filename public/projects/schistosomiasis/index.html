Applied Bioinformatics Schistosomiasis Research

September 2024 · Katherine McCallum

Description#

This project aims to validate current research on schistosomiasis parasitic liver worms. The current research is done on Liver worms because they are easier to sample and analyze. The issue is that the liver worms do not transmit the disease, the intestinal worms are the ones to exit the host and infect a new host. If the gene expression between a liver and intestinal worm is significantly different, this could indicate that all current research is useless and needs to use intestinal worms to be valid and informative.
